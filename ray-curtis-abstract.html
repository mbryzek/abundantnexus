Ray Curtis Abstract

Serial Entrepreneurship: Lessons in Innovation and Resilience

Curtis Ray, Omnitron Sensors

Curtis Ray's 20-minute talk offers a pragmatic yet inspiring look at serial entrepreneurship through the lens of MEMS technology. Drawing from his own experiences, Curtis explores the vital roles of perspective, resilience, and deep industry knowledge in driving entrepreneurial success. He shares how shifting attitudes transformed his early work experiences and discusses the competitive edge and innovation gained from embracing an underdog status, as learned at Purdue's rowing team and in the face-off against industry giants like HP, OnSemi and Siemens. While acknowledging the 

influence of figures like Dr. Janusz Bryzek, this talk focuses on the broader narrative of entrepreneurial spirit, underlining the importance of adaptability and expertise in the fast-evolving tech landscape. Attendees will gain insights into turning challenges into opportunities, emphasizing practical strategies for thriving in today's dynamic business world.
